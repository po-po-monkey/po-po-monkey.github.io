About The Game

Po-Po Monkey: Neon Nexus is an innovative cyberpunk maze game where players guide augmented penguins through intricate digital labyrinths to collect data nodes while navigating dynamic control schemes that change during gameplay.

Set in beautifully rendered neon-drenched environments (now with an orange overdrive!), the game challenges players to adapt quickly as control configurations shift between six different modes. Whether playing against AI opponents or competing in multiplayer matches, each game offers a fresh strategic challenge.

Customize your cyber-penguin with unlockable skins and cyberware, climb leaderboards in competitive tournaments, and earn in-game crypto tokens that can be used for upgrades or trading on the blockchain.

Control Schemes

Master six different cybernetic control configurations that change during gameplay. Adapt quickly to maintain your advantage in the digital maze!

Diamond

  • UP: Up
  • DOWN: Down
  • LEFT: Left
  • RIGHT: Right

Triangle

  • UP: Down
  • DOWN: Up
  • LEFT: Left
  • RIGHT: Right

Rectangle

  • UP: Up
  • DOWN: Down
  • LEFT: Right
  • RIGHT: Left

Star

  • UP: Left
  • DOWN: Right
  • LEFT: Up
  • RIGHT: Down

Circle

  • UP: Down
  • DOWN: Up
  • LEFT: Right
  • RIGHT: Left

Pentagon

  • UP: Right
  • DOWN: Left
  • LEFT: Down
  • RIGHT: Up
